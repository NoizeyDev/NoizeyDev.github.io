What is Beardy's Valor?

Beardy's Valor is my first attempt at a Skyrim modlist. As a long term player, I have always enjoyed the story of the game. And almost with equal measure found it to be one of the most frustrating to try and work around. What do I want from this modlist? Performant, but beautiful, with a heavy focus on animations and mechanics, but keeping lore as intact as possible.
MCO | ADXP, SCAR, OAR. Goetia, Vanguard, Smooth Moveset. Both 1st and 3rd person.
Not on a top end PC? Only interested in Next Gen textures? Don't worry, I have you covered. High performance & High Detail profiles available

Currently in early development
