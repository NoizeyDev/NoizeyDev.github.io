What is Beardy's Valor?


Beardy's Valor is my first attempt at a Skyrim modlist.
As a long time player, I have always enjoyed the story of the game but have been victim of boredom with its repetitive action.
The modding community have done an amazing job pulling together and tackling the nuances about this game that grind on the player. And I intend to compliment their work by curating this modlist.

What do I want from this modlist? Performance and beauty, but with a heavy focus on animations and mechanics. I want the game to feel as fresh as possible, whilst retaining the magic that the game released with.



MCO | ADXP


The advancements with moveesets have been impressive over the last few years, and in v1.0 ill be building the entire framework around MCO | ADXP.
It has been a fascinating project and i feel confident I can curate the collections to suit any play style.



SCAR - OAR - Goetia - Vanguard - Smooth Moveset - SIGMA


This modlist includes both 1st and 3rd person animations.
The work put out by SIGMA in their 1st person animations has really lifted my gameplay as of late. And as someone who enjoys flipping between 1st and 3rd person, I knew this had to be included.
Verolevi's work with Goetia, Leviathan and Vanguard has been a staple go to for many modders, expect them in this list.
I will be creating custom OAR conditionals for this list, and expanding this with SCAR AI and Stances. I have no intention of releasing this list with stock conditions. Expect a new experience with each click

Not on a top end PC? Only interested in Next Gen textures?
Don't worry, I have you covered. High performance & High Detail profiles available
